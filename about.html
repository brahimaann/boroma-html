A brief introduction,

After taking photos for a few years now within the realm of the visual arts, Brahima finds himself enjoying the art of story telling. As a photographer and artist he attempts to emphasize the importance of our individual stories and how they define each one of us in our own unique way while simoutaneously weaving us into an interconnected narrative.

Always open to learning along the way, Brahima incorporates that mentality into his work by always trying to expand his understanding of the world and its people.
